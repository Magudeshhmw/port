About TamilNadu Trekker

Welcome to TamilNadu Trekker, your gateway to exploring the natural wonders of Tamil Nadu. As a passionate trekker and nature enthusiast, I document my journeys through the diverse landscapes of this beautiful state.

From the misty Western Ghats to the serene Eastern Ghats, from dense forests to cascading waterfalls, my adventures take me through some of the most breathtaking terrains in South India.

Through this platform, I aim to share my experiences, provide valuable information to fellow trekkers, and promote sustainable eco-tourism in Tamil Nadu.
